Apel Wealth Halal Investment Product

The APELWM Halal Investment Product (“APELWMHIP” or “The Product”) is an investment product targeted at Retail, High-Networth Individuals (HNIs) and Qualified Institutional Investors (QIIs) seeking investment opportunities with attractive returns that align with their faith and ethical beliefs.

FEATURES


• Safety due to the characteristics of the underlying assets

• Higher competitive returns relative to available Short-Term Shariah-Complaint Investments

• Short-term income generation

• Tenors: 90 days to 365 days.

• Minimum subscription: N1 Million



OBJECTIVES


• Seeking to align their investments to their faith and ethical beliefs

• Requiring income generation

• Desirous of Competitive Return on Investment

• Seeking capital preservation
